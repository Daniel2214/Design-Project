Le Bristol Paris

Le Bristol is elegant, stylish, extremely comfortable and on Faubourg St. Honore in the exclusive heart of Paris. Service is excellent, friendly and also discreet which is appreciated by the many film and fashion celebrities who always return to this haven . Rooms are large and beautifully decorated, very tranquil and comfortable. Gorgeous big bathrooms with La Prairie products and a La Prairie Spa on first floor. Perfect place to stay in the city of light.

Le 123 Sebastopol

A really smart boutique hotel based on the film industry. The public areas are spotlessly clean and tidy as are the bedrooms. The hotel is interesting based on the cinema and films so the rooms are based on film making. The building has been sympathetically modernised. The staff are first class always polite, helpful and friendly and efficient. The bedrooms are well equipped, very clean and room 511 had a shower which had room to swing a cat in.
